# Business Insights

## 1. E-Commerce Growth

Order volume increased significantly between 2016 and 2018, indicating rapid growth in online shopping adoption across Brazil.

---

## 2. Peak Customer Activity

Most customers placed orders during afternoon hours, suggesting that customer engagement and promotional activities may perform better during that period.

---

## 3. Regional Sales Concentration

São Paulo (SP) contributed the highest number of customers and overall order value, making it the strongest market region in the dataset.

---

## 4. Freight Cost Variability

Certain states experienced much higher freight costs, likely influenced by geographical distance and logistics complexity.

---

## 5. Delivery Performance Challenges

Some northern states showed higher average delivery times, indicating possible operational and transportation bottlenecks.

---

## 6. Payment Preferences

Credit cards were the most preferred payment method, while installment-based payments were widely used among customers.

---

## 7. Operational Opportunities

The analysis highlights opportunities for:

- Logistics optimization
- Delivery efficiency improvements
- Freight cost reduction
- Better regional inventory planning
