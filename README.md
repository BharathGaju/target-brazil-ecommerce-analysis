# Target Brazil E-Commerce Data Analysis

## Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Target Brazil’s e-commerce operations using SQL. The dataset contains over 100,000 orders placed between 2016 and 2018 and includes information related to customers, payments, products, freight costs, delivery timelines, and reviews.

The main objective of this analysis is to understand customer purchasing behavior, regional sales patterns, delivery performance, and payment trends to derive meaningful business insights.

---

## Business Problem

Target Brazil wants to better understand how its e-commerce business performed across different regions and customer segments.

Using transactional and operational data, this analysis attempts to answer questions such as:

- How did order volumes grow over time?
- Which states contributed the most revenue?
- When do customers place most of their orders?
- Which regions experienced higher delivery delays?
- How do freight charges vary across states?
- What payment methods are commonly used?

The insights from this analysis can help improve logistics planning, customer experience, and operational efficiency.

## Key Insights

### Order Growth Trend

The number of orders increased significantly from 2016 to 2018, indicating rapid growth in e-commerce adoption.

### Peak Ordering Time

Most customers placed orders during the afternoon, suggesting higher shopping activity during those hours.

### Customer Distribution

São Paulo (SP) had the highest number of customers and contributed the highest overall order value among all states.

### Freight Analysis

Freight charges varied considerably across states, with some regions showing much higher average freight costs.

### Delivery Performance

Certain northern states experienced longer average delivery times compared to other regions, highlighting possible logistical challenges.

### Payment Trends

Credit cards were the most commonly used payment method, and a large number of customers preferred installment-based payments.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Improve Delivery Efficiency

States with consistently high delivery times can be prioritized for logistics optimization and warehouse expansion.

### Optimize Marketing Campaign Timing

Since customer activity is highest during afternoon hours, promotional campaigns can be scheduled during peak engagement periods.

### Reduce Freight Costs

Analyzing high freight-cost regions may help identify opportunities for route optimization and better shipping partnerships.

### Expand Flexible Payment Options

The popularity of installment payments suggests that flexible payment methods are important for customer conversion.
